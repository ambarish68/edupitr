How do I start the review process ?

  1. Send documents and brief profile description

    E-mail ID: review.edupitr@gmail.com
    Mail Title: ‘Name of student’,’Document’,’Edupitr Service Chosen’.
    Content: GRE, TOEFL scorecards, resume and profile description.
    Include application deadline to help us prioritize tasks.
    Please send your documents in Word format.

  2. Make payment

    Depending on the customization and number of iterations required, we will let you know the cost of service via e-mail.
    Use 'Subscribe' link above to make payment before we can start working on tailoring your documents.

  3. You are all set!

    Sit back and relax as alumni will work on your documents.

Note:
  • One 1 document means 1 SOP OR 1 LOR OR 1 resume. Multiple SOPs OR LORs do not mean 1 document.
  • Except for Edupitr Expedite, please allow us 2-3 days for a response on document review.
  • Document review starts after the payment is made.
  • Depending on the service you choose and your admission timeline, we can work together to get things done on time.
