Our story

Not long back as we recollect our journey to study abroad, we began by seeking the most celebrated counselor in the city to help us get into our dream university. The overall experience was not the best one.
  • First of all, the consultation fees seemed exorbitant.
  • Secondly, the 'consultant' was not from the relevant technical background. For instance, my consultant would not understand how to present my VLSI design internship at IIT Bombay to get into Data Science or Machine Learning specialization at Carnegie Mellon University for research track/job?
  • There was ‘one-advice-to-all’ strategy by consultants for most of the friends. Naturally, I wonder how can I stand out from of the crowd?
What helped us most was the advice from previous alumni from our dream universities whom we aspired to follow. It is impossible for a student to have connections in most universities which help them stand out in the pool of applicants. So here we are... Right from understanding the actual situation for research/job, possible funding opportunities and much more. Inception !!!
